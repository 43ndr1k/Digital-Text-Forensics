The paper shows the importance of an interdisciplinary analysis of linguistic features that enable
authorship detection or author profiling in forensic, literary, and in economic context (anonymous
threats, literary texts of unknown authorship, client profiling). It also highlights the lack of realised
analyses for Slovene, and outlines the methodology of extracting syntactic, lexical, semantic, and
character features in order to quantify the author’s personal style in Slovene.
