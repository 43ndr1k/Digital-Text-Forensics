ABSTRACT 
 
Recently, extracting the demographic information like age, 
gender and race by using speech and face attributes takes 
much attention in the literature. In this research, we have 
focused on the implementation of a demographic 
information classification system and proved the 
relationship between spoken language and demographic 
profile of people. In the first step, the feature vectors of 
spoken language were extracted then dimensions of the 
feature vectors were reduced by our feature reduction 
method and Correlation Based Feature Selection method. 
Finally, the success of Naïve Bayes, Support Vector 
Machine and K-Nearest Neigbour classification 
algorithms was evaluated.
